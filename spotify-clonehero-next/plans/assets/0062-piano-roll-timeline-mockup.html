Piano-roll timeline — tempo markers & context menus

The x-axis is real time: the waveform never moves, the grid does. Tempo markers (◆) exist only where the tempo changes; drag one to refit the grid to the audio — the BPM of the segments on either side recalculates and its neighbors stay put. Right-click the tempo lane to add a marker at the nearest beat or toggle a downbeat; right-click a marker to delete it. Right-click notes to switch tom/cymbal or delete.

The Notes toggle picks what notes do when the grid moves: glued to audio (ticks recompute, notes stay on the recording — the transcription default) or glued to grid (notes ride their gridlines).
